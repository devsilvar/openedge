Sub-Text
Open Edge trains and deploys critical middle-management and operational talent to ensure 
hospitality businesses work efficiently and profitably.
Who This Training Is For
This training is designed for hospitality professionals who:
• Currently work (or want to work) in operations or middle management
• Are tired of chaotic workplaces with no systems
• Want to be taken seriously as operational leaders
• Are willing to learn, be accountable, and do the work properly
• Want to grow into roles where they are trusted to run the business
This training is NOT for:
• People looking for certificates without responsibility
• Those unwilling to uphold standards
• Anyone expecting shortcuts or entitlement
Roles We Train
• Head of Operations
• Housekeeping Supervisor
• Front Office Supervisor
• Facility and Maintenance Supervisor
• Cost Control Officer
These are the roles that determine whether a hospitality business works or collapses.
Why Open Edge Training Is Different
Most hospitality trainings focus on theory.
Open Edge focuses on how businesses actually operate in Nigeria.
We train you to:
• Understand systems, not just tasks
• Work with owners professionally
• Manage people under pressure
• Protect revenue and enforce controls
• Lead without chaos
Strong people fail in weak systems.
We train you to operate within strong ones.
Training Format
• Intensive Physical Training (Practical, focused, no distractions)
• 20 Hours Online (Operational thinking, leadership, controls)
This is hands-on and demanding. Attendance and participation matter.

• Immediate operational impact on real businesses
• Clear understanding of your role and growth path
• Exposure to structured, ethical hospitality operations
This training exists to build professionals whom owners can trust, not just employees.
