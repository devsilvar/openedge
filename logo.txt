# OPEN EDGE - BRAND INFORMATION FOR LOGO DESIGN

## BUSINESS OVERVIEW

**Company Name:** Open Edge (also known as "Dubor")

**Tagline/Mission:** 
"We fix hospitality businesses so owners can build wealth and leave a lasting legacy, while staff grow their careers."

**Core Purpose:**
Open Edge is a hospitality solutions firm in Nigeria that fixes system and middle-management failures holding hospitality businesses back. They help hospitality owners move from chaos to profitability, making businesses sustainable and transferable as legacy assets.

---

## WHAT THEY DO

1. **Business Diagnostics & Health Checks**
   - Assess hospitality businesses to identify system
   - Identify failures middle-management issues
   - Provide clarity and solutions for business problems

2. **Operating System Implementation**
   - Practical 90-day intensive program to start up or reposition hospitality businesses
   - Fix systems, align teams, make business profitable
   - Create transferable, sustainable businesses

3. **Leadership Development & Training**
   - Train and deploy critical middle-management talent
   - Develop operational leaders who can run hospitality businesses properly
   - Career growth programs for hospitality professionals

---

## TARGET AUDIENCE

**Primary:**
- Hospitality business owners in Nigeria (particularly Lagos & Abuja)
- Owners who want to build wealth and leave lasting legacy

**Secondary:**
- Hotel/restaurant managers seeking career advancement
- Hospitality operators wanting proper training

**Market Position:**
- Premium establishments (50+ businesses served in Lagos & Abuja)
- B2B service - they work with businesses, not individual consumers
